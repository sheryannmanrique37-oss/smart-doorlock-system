System Setup & Overview

1. Initial Connect Mode

When the system first turns on without internet, it automatically creates a Wi-Fi setup network. Simply connect your mobile phone to the "SmartDoor_001" Wi-Fi and a setup page will immediately load enabling you to attach it to your actual local house WiFi network natively!

2. Remote Hardware Enrollment

To enroll a completely new physical identity, click either "Remote OTA Fingerprint" or "Scan RFID on Door". This temporarily activates the door scanner for registration. The screen on the door will guide the user to tap their card or place their finger. The system instantly captures that credential natively!

3. Registering Final Profiles

After the physical scan completes, click "Add Final User" (or wait for the auto-fill after an RFID scan). Fill out the Associate Name, select their clearance level, and map the recently assigned hardware ID. You can also enforce specific working hour time restrictions from this profile!
